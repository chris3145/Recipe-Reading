<p class="entry-meta"><time class="entry-time" itemprop="datePublished" datetime="2015-06-14T07:00:38+00:00">June 14, 2015</time> by <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/Person"><a href="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/author/cremedelacrumb1/" class="entry-author-link" itemprop="url" rel="author"><span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">Tiffany</span></a></span> <span class="entry-comments-link"><a href="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos/#comments">9 Comments</a></span> </p></header><div class="entry-content" itemprop="text"><div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper"><a href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.lecremedelacrumb.com%2Fslow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos%2F&media=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.lecremedelacrumb.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2015%2F06%2Fslow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-8.jpg&description=Slow%20cooked%20ranch%20chicken%20wrapped%20in%20soft%20flour%20tortillas%20and%20topped%20with%20red%20onion%20and%20cabbage%20slaw%2C%20cilantro%2C%20and%20creamy%20ranch%20dressing%21" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" data-pin-config="none" data-pin-height="32" data-pin-color="gray"  data-pin-shape="round" rel="nobox"><img src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It" data-pib-button="true" /></a></div><div class="border-round"></div><p style="text-align: center;"><em>Slow Cooker Ranch Chicken Tacos &#8211; Slow cooked ranch chicken wrapped in soft flour tortillas and topped with red onion and cabbage slaw, cilantro, and creamy ranch dressing! </em></p>
<p><a href="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/?attachment_id=11130" rel="attachment wp-att-11130"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11130 pib-hover-img" src="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-1.jpg" alt="Slow Cooker Ranch Chicken Tacos | Creme de la Crumb" width="680" height="1020" srcset="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-1.jpg 680w, http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-1-200x300.jpg 200w, http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-1-300x450.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 680px) 100vw, 680px" data-pin-url="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos/" /></a></p>
<p>It&#8217;s Taco Thursday! As of&#8230; now.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/?attachment_id=11133" rel="attachment wp-att-11133"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11133 pib-hover-img" src="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-4.jpg" alt="Slow Cooker Ranch Chicken Tacos | Creme de la Crumb" width="680" height="1020" srcset="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-4.jpg 680w, http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-4-200x300.jpg 200w, http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-4-300x450.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 680px) 100vw, 680px" data-pin-url="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos/" /></a></p>
<p><span id="more-11129"></span>You guys, I have tacos at least once every single week. I&#8217;m really not kidding though when I say <strong>at least</strong> once.  I&#8217;ve had them three times this week and It&#8217;s only Thursday. Do you realize that means I&#8217;ve had some variety of taco every day of the week so far??</p>
<p>I&#8217;m not obsessed. It&#8217;s just really really really like them and sometimes I shed a tear or two if a taco doesn&#8217;t meet my expectations or (heaven forbid) a taco I was planning on and sooo looking forward to doesn&#8217;t happen at all.</p>
<p>That&#8217;s not obsessed right?</p>
<p><a href="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/?attachment_id=11136" rel="attachment wp-att-11136"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11136 pib-hover-img" src="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-7.jpg" alt="Slow Cooker Ranch Chicken Tacos | Creme de la Crumb" width="680" height="1020" srcset="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-7.jpg 680w, http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-7-200x300.jpg 200w, http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-7-300x450.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 680px) 100vw, 680px" data-pin-url="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos/" /></a></p>
<p>I think it&#8217;s the filling options. Because you can have a sandwich &#8211; a bunch of meat and cheese and veggies, slathered in sauce and stuffed between two slices of bread &#8211; and it&#8217;s kiiiiind of like a taco. Sort of. They&#8217;re like&#8230;. food cousins maybe? But the differences  are key.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/?attachment_id=11135" rel="attachment wp-att-11135"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11135 pib-hover-img" src="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-6.jpg" alt="Slow Cooker Ranch Chicken Tacos | Creme de la Crumb" width="680" height="1020" srcset="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-6.jpg 680w, http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-6-200x300.jpg 200w, http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-6-300x450.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 680px) 100vw, 680px" data-pin-url="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos/" /></a></p>
<p>For one thing the meat usually (I said usually) comes thinly sliced in a sandwich and in a taco it is usually chunks or shredded. For the veggies, well, I suppose those can be the same whether it&#8217;s on bread or in a tortilla. But the sauces! I think that&#8217;s the real kicker. The sauces and flavor variety that goes into a taco is often a lot different than what you would have in a sandwich. And I suppose I favor the taco flavors.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/?attachment_id=11132" rel="attachment wp-att-11132"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11132 pib-hover-img" src="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-3.jpg" alt="Slow Cooker Ranch Chicken Tacos | Creme de la Crumb" width="680" height="1020" srcset="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-3.jpg 680w, http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-3-200x300.jpg 200w, http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-3-300x450.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 680px) 100vw, 680px" data-pin-url="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos/" /></a></p>
<p>But today, I&#8217;m switching things up with our taco fillings and especially the sauce. I&#8217;m 100% on board with any kind of taco topped with salsa (I heart salsa forevah) but we&#8217;re gonna set aside that jar of On the Border for a second and think creamy, white RANCH.</p>
<p>Sound crazy? It is. Crazy DELICIOUS.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/?attachment_id=11134" rel="attachment wp-att-11134"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11134 pib-hover-img" src="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-5.jpg" alt="Slow Cooker Ranch Chicken Tacos | Creme de la Crumb" width="680" height="1020" srcset="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-5.jpg 680w, http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-5-200x300.jpg 200w, http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-5-300x450.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 680px) 100vw, 680px" data-pin-url="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos/" /></a></p>
<p>These ranch chicken tacos are the easiest and most delightful thing in the history of ever. All you do is slow cook some chicken breasts in your crockpot for a few hours with some water and ranch seasoning (yeah, from the packet!), then shred, stuff, and top with prepared ranch dressing, slaw, avocado, and cilantro. It&#8217;s heaven I tell you, heaven!</p>
<p>Here, this one&#8217;s for you&#8230;</p>
<p><a href="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/?attachment_id=11137" rel="attachment wp-att-11137"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11137 pib-hover-img" src="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-8.jpg" alt="Slow Cooker Ranch Chicken Tacos | Creme de la Crumb" width="680" height="1020" srcset="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-8.jpg 680w, http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-8-200x300.jpg 200w, http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos-8-300x450.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 680px) 100vw, 680px" data-pin-url="http://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/slow-cooker-ranch-chicken-tacos/" /></a><br />
