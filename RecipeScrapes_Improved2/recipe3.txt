		<div class="entry-content">
			<p><a href="https://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/roastedveggies1.jpg"><img src="https://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/roastedveggies1.jpg?w=780" alt="roastedveggies"   class="alignnone size-full wp-image-16027 frame-img width_630" /></a>I was in the big city last week, and I decided about an hour into my errands that I was hungry. So I stopped at <a href="http://pettysfinefoods.com/" target="_blank">a lovely grocery store</a> that&#8217;s been in Tulsa forever, because they have a little section of to-go foods that I love, and I can usually find something delicious that isn&#8217;t a burger. I love burgers and all, but when I go to civilization and need to grab something quick to eat, a burger isn&#8217;t at the top on my list. I can always get one in our small town! Or, of course&#8230;I can make one at home. We live on a ranch, man.</p>
<p>One of the 700 things I chose at the market was a little package of gorgeous roasted vegetables. We all know how to roast vegetables, of course, but I thought this assortment was so uniquely beautiful, and I loved that they really didn&#8217;t care about all the vegetables came from the same season. Spring vegetables were mixed with fall vegetables, dogs and cats were living together, and it turned out to be utterly delicious.</p>
<p>Yesterday I made a batch! Here&#8217;s the step-by-step recipe.</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15451284092" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15451284092_a51f244fce_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>Slice some asparagus into pieces: I cut off the tips, then the center section, then I discard the rough ends. </p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15265066797" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15265066797_d97c04eb7d_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>Then I throw the asparagus into a big bowl.</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15428559326" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15428559326_f21df82f8a_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>Then I peeled a red onion&#8230;</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15428558586" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15428558586_be409afd73_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>Cut it into wedges&#8230;</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15448471321" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15448471321_11f8a5a3fd_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>Broke them into pieces&#8230;</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15451631035" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15451631035_35af8d35d8_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>And threw them in the bowl. </p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15265019588" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15265019588_ff34e374ca_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>I lopped off the top and bottom of a red bell pepper, then chopped it into large, rough pieces&#8230;</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15265018978" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15265018978_344bb95747_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>And threw those in. The colors are getting gorgeous!</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15264861179" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15264861179_82bc5ff2d3_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>Next up: mushrooms! If they were big, I cut them into quarters&#8230;if they were not so big, I cut them in half.</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15451629165" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15451629165_a27c2490d0_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>And just threw them into the beautiful mix.</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15448467631" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15448467631_31cd98029f_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>Now, if you think you don&#8217;t like eggplant, please give me just a moment of your time. I beg you, I implore you&#8212;please try one in this dish. Roasted eggplant is one of life&#8217;s sheer pleasures, and it converts even the staunchest anti-eggplant humans. Just slice it into thick rounds&#8230;</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15264859709" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15264859709_35f2f851e3_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>Then cut each round into chunks. I usually discard the center piece because I like each bite of eggplant to have the beautiful skin on it.</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15428554446" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15428554446_54d5bb9823_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>Throw it into the bowl. You&#8217;re gonna love it!</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15265060447" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15265060447_171cb1e72c_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>I loved the pieces of butternut squash in the veggie assortment: It was gorgeous, and roasted butternut squash makes my skirt fly up in a major way.</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15264923550" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15264923550_dbc8047c77_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>Peel off the hard skin&#8230;</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15265015028" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15265015028_a9fbacdc31_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>Then cut off the narrower top portion. It&#8217;s the perfect amount! (Save the other half in a ziploc in the fridge.)</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15451625325" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15451625325_6cb6be5987_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>Cut the hunk into strips, then cut the strips into large chunks&#8230;</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15451624705" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15451624705_166c3f279d_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>And throw them in.</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15264921100" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15264921100_5c8e2eb866_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>For majah flavah, mince up some garlic&#8230;</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15264920710" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15264920710_5e4682a1d0_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>And throw it in, too. </p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15265012158" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15265012158_b305c93aa4_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>Toss it all together and behold the gorgeousness! Love this combination. And actually, I forgot to add carrots&#8212;the assortment at the grocery store had them. It also had small potatoes, which I chose to leave out. Other than that, pretty much the same!</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15264919500" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15264919500_a1ce9d0af7_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>Now, drizzle in some olive oil&#8230;</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15265054357" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15265054357_e7966521f0_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>Then some salt&#8230;</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15265053767" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15265053767_64ac2738b8_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>Pepper&#8230;</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15264916890" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15264916890_a18bf8de0c_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>And whatever other seasonings or herbs you&#8217;d like!</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15451619595" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15451619595_d44fe46c39_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>I&#8217;ll tell you the crazy thing I used if you promise not to tell anyone: Montreal steak seasoning. Ha. It&#8217;s delicious!</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15265051707" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15265051707_3a90931a74_o.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>Now just toss it all together to make sure the olive oil and seasonings are coating everything&#8230;</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15428544026" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15428544026_f1c2296448_z.jpg?w=426&#038;h=640" width="426" height="640" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_426 frame-img" /></a>Then get them onto two baking sheets and shake them to spread everything out (I didn&#8217;t show the spread-everything-out step.) I use two baking sheets because I don&#8217;t want to crowd the veggies. If they&#8217;re crowded, they won&#8217;t roast as easily and beautifully.</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15428542966" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15428542966_018c313c20_z.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>I roasted them at 450 degrees (yes, 450 degrees!) for about 20 minutes, shaking the pans at least twice during the cooking process so the veggies wouldn&#8217;t stick and burn. Be sure to check the veggies as they cook, and just keep going until they&#8217;re nice and lovely with dark bits around the edges.</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15451264742" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15451264742_5149f5be9a_z.jpg?w=426&#038;h=640" width="426" height="640" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_426 frame-img" /></a>Gorgeous!</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15265003848" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15265003848_501133d60e_z.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>This is how spread out they were as they roasted. They needed space, man.</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15264844489" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15264844489_5bfd146f88_z.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>My daughter Alex was getting ready to head to town to hang out with her BFF Meg, so I piled half the veggies onto a smaller baking sheet&#8230;</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15451261262" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15451261262_518229a94a_z.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>Because I knew Meg&#8217;s mom, Hyacinth, would love these.</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15451611785" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15451611785_48e335089e_z.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>Here&#8217;s what you can do with these: Serve them as a side dish, pile them on a sandwich or inside a panini, or puree them and make soup.</p>
<p>&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/15265043857" title="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables by Ree Drummond, on Flickr"><img src="http://pioneerwoman.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/15265043857_baa79ea3cf_z.jpg?w=630&#038;h=419" width="630" height="419" alt="Beautiful Roasted Vegetables" class="width_630 frame-img" /></a>And that&#8217;s exactly what I told Hyacinth. (Update: She called me this morning to tell me she&#8217;s making soup!)</p>
<p>Here&#8217;s the handy dandy printable. Enjoy these gorgeous veggies! They keep really well in the fridge, so you can munch on them all week.</p>
