											<h1 class="gdlr-blog-title">Creamy Sausage Spaghetti</h1>
						
					
					<div class="gdlr-blog-info gdlr-title-font gdlr-info"><span class="gdlr-seperator">/</span><div class="blog-info blog-comments"><i class="icon-comments-alt"></i><a href="http://www.mantitlement.com/recipes/creamy-sausage-spaghetti/#respond" >10</a></div><span class="gdlr-seperator">/</span><div class="blog-info blog-category"><i class="icon-folder-close-alt"></i><a href="http://www.mantitlement.com/category/recipes/main-dishes/" rel="tag">Main Dishes</a><span class="sep">,</span> <a href="http://www.mantitlement.com/category/recipes/" rel="tag">Recipes</a></div><div class="clear"></div></div><div class="gdlr-blog-info gdlr-title-font gdlr-info gdlr-blog-full-date"><div class="gdlr-blog-info gdlr-title-font gdlr-info"><div class="blog-info blog-date"><i class="icon-calendar-empty"></i><a href="http://www.mantitlement.com/2016/09/28/">28 Sep 2016</a></div><div class="clear"></div></div></div>			
					<div class="clear"></div>
				</header><!-- entry-header -->

				<div class="gdlr-blog-content"><p>Creamy Sausage Spaghetti will become one of your favorite pasta dinners! The trick is that the sausage is in every single bite!</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-10164" src="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/creamy-sausage-spaghetti-top-image.jpg" alt="Creamy Sausage Spaghetti with sausage and peas is going to become your new favorite dinner!" width="750" height="1100" srcset="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/creamy-sausage-spaghetti-top-image.jpg 750w, http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/creamy-sausage-spaghetti-top-image-205x300.jpg 205w, http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/creamy-sausage-spaghetti-top-image-698x1024.jpg 698w" sizes="(max-width: 750px) 100vw, 750px" /></p>
<p>I definitely have an opinion about the ratio of ingredients in recipes.</p>
<p>It needs to be right. A sandwich can taste totally different if theres too much of one ingredient on it, even when the ingredients are the same. Right?</p>
<p>That&#8217;s what I&#8217;m talking about.</p>
<p>This Creamy Sausage Spaghetti is a perfect example of that!</p>
<p>Not one bite of pasta while your fishing around for the sausage, the sausage is more part of the sauce, crumbled up very small so it goes all in every bite. Like this <a href="http://www.mantitlement.com/recipes/garlic-bread-pasta/" rel="nofollow">Garlic Bread Pasta</a> &#8211; one of my favorites.</p>
<p>OK, so now that I think you&#8217;ve got the idea &#8211; here&#8217;s how you make it.</p>
<p>Brown the sausage and then transfer it to a food processor. Yep, food processor. No way you can crumble it this fine with a spoon &#8211; the machine can do it better.</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-10160" src="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/creamy-sausage-spaghetti-image.jpg" alt="Creamy Sausage Spaghetti starts with the sausage pulsed in a food processor for sausage in every bite!" width="750" height="1100" srcset="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/creamy-sausage-spaghetti-image.jpg 750w, http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/creamy-sausage-spaghetti-image-205x300.jpg 205w, http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/creamy-sausage-spaghetti-image-698x1024.jpg 698w" sizes="(max-width: 750px) 100vw, 750px" /></p>
<p>Now add the sausage back to the same skillet with the onions, garlic and sun dried tomatoes.</p>
<p>Pour in crushed tomatoes and stir it all together.</p>
<p>You&#8217;ll need to cook it down for about 20 minutes or so, but in the meantime you can boil the spaghetti and drain it so it&#8217;s ready and waiting for you when the sauce is done.</p>
<p>Pour in the cream&#8230;this is CREAMY Sausage Spaghetti after all&#8230;then add a few handfuls of frozen peas.</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-10158" src="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/creamy-sausage-spaghetti-cream-photo.jpg" alt="Creamy Sausage Spaghetti is loaded with sausage in a creamy tomato sauce!" width="750" height="1100" srcset="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/creamy-sausage-spaghetti-cream-photo.jpg 750w, http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/creamy-sausage-spaghetti-cream-photo-205x300.jpg 205w, http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/creamy-sausage-spaghetti-cream-photo-698x1024.jpg 698w" sizes="(max-width: 750px) 100vw, 750px" /></p>
<p>Only a few more minutes and you&#8217;re on your way to this.</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-10163" src="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/creamy-sausage-spaghetti-tongs-image.jpg" alt="Creamy Sausage Spaghetti has tons of sausage in every single bite!" width="750" height="1100" srcset="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/creamy-sausage-spaghetti-tongs-image.jpg 750w, http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/creamy-sausage-spaghetti-tongs-image-205x300.jpg 205w, http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/creamy-sausage-spaghetti-tongs-image-698x1024.jpg 698w" sizes="(max-width: 750px) 100vw, 750px" /></p>
<p>This Creamy Sausage Spaghetti is the pasta dinner that you won&#8217;t be able to stop eating.</p>
<p>Like the push away from the table because your pants don&#8217;t fit anymore spaghetti dinner. It&#8217;s that good.</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-10159" src="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/creamy-sausage-spaghetti-featured-photo.jpg" alt="Creamy Sausage Spaghetti is loaded with sausage in a creamy tomato sauce!" width="750" height="1100" srcset="http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/creamy-sausage-spaghetti-featured-photo.jpg 750w, http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/creamy-sausage-spaghetti-featured-photo-205x300.jpg 205w, http://www.mantitlement.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/creamy-sausage-spaghetti-featured-photo-698x1024.jpg 698w" sizes="(max-width: 750px) 100vw, 750px" /></p>
<p>But I can promise you, it&#8217;s totally worth it!</p>
