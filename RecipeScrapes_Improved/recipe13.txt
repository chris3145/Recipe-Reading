<p id="mpprecipe-instructions" class="h-4 strong">Instructions</p><ol id="mpprecipe-instructions-list" class="instructions"><li id="mpprecipe-instruction-0" class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Heat skillet over medium high heat</li><li id="mpprecipe-instruction-1" class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Place milk and bread crumbs in separate shallow bowls</li><li id="mpprecipe-instruction-2" class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Dip chicken in milk, and then coat with crumbs</li><li id="mpprecipe-instruction-3" class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Add chicken to skillet with 1/2 of the butter about 5 min</li><li id="mpprecipe-instruction-4" class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Add remaining butter flip chicken and cook another 5-8 min or until chicken is cooked.</li><li id="mpprecipe-instruction-5" class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Remove and set aside</li><li id="mpprecipe-instruction-6" class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Add broth to skillet bring to boil over medium heat</li><li id="mpprecipe-instruction-7" class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Stir in cream, pimientos, and tomatoes; boil and stir for 1 min</li><li id="mpprecipe-instruction-8" class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Reduce heat, add parmesan cheese, bousrin cheese, basil and pepper; cook and stir until heated through.</li><li id="mpprecipe-instruction-9" class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Add the chicken back into the sauce.</li><li id="mpprecipe-instruction-10" class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Serve chicken as is, covered in sauce, or with rice or pasta.</li><li id="mpprecipe-instruction-11" class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Enjoy!</li></ol><div class="mealplannerpro-recipe-plugin" style="display: none;">6.6.15</div><a id="mpp-printed-permalink" href="http://www.thecookierookie.com/skillet-basil-cream-chicken-2/"title="Permalink to Recipe">http://www.thecookierookie.com/skillet-basil-cream-chicken-2/</a></div><link class='photo' itemprop='image' href='http://www.thecookierookie.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/skillet-basil-cream-chicken-feature-300x300.jpg' />
