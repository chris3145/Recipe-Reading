<p>It&#8217;s Italian night for Meals Made Easy! So of course we will need to start with some bread sticks. I have you covered with these super yummy <a href="http://www.goodenessgracious.com/2010/11/goode-gooseberry-project-parmesan-bread-sticks.html">Parmesan Garlic Bread Sticks</a>!</p>
<p><a href="http://www.goodenessgracious.com/2010/11/goode-gooseberry-project-parmesan-bread-sticks.html"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8797" alt="Parmesean Garlic Bred Sticks" src="http://www.goodenessgracious.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Parmesean-Garlic-Bred-Sticks.jpg" width="400" height="266" srcset="http://www.goodenessgracious.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Parmesean-Garlic-Bred-Sticks.jpg 400w, http://www.goodenessgracious.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Parmesean-Garlic-Bred-Sticks-300x200.jpg 300w, http://www.goodenessgracious.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Parmesean-Garlic-Bred-Sticks-200x133.jpg 200w" sizes="(max-width: 400px) 100vw, 400px" /></a></p>
<p>You will also need some salad dressing to go with your salad and bread sticks. Do you like Olive Garden&#8217;s salad dressing? Stupid question, I know&#8230;who doesn&#8217;t like their dressing?! Brandie, aka The Country Cook, has you covered with her recipe for <a href="http://www.thecountrycook.net/2012/01/olive-garden-salad-dressing.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.thecountrycook.net/2012/01/olive-garden-salad-dressing.html', 'Olive Garden Salad Dressing');">Olive Garden Salad Dressing</a>!</p>
<p><a href="http://www.thecountrycook.net/2012/01/olive-garden-salad-dressing.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.thecountrycook.net/2012/01/olive-garden-salad-dressing.html', '');"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8798" alt="Olive Garden Salad Dressing" src="http://www.goodenessgracious.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Olive-Garden-Salad-Dressing.jpg" width="300" height="400" srcset="http://www.goodenessgracious.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Olive-Garden-Salad-Dressing.jpg 300w, http://www.goodenessgracious.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Olive-Garden-Salad-Dressing-225x300.jpg 225w, http://www.goodenessgracious.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Olive-Garden-Salad-Dressing-150x200.jpg 150w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></a></p>
<p>Over at Lauren&#8217;s Latest, she has a super cute way to make lasagna that the kids are sure to love! <a href="http://www.laurenslatest.com/lasagna-cups/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.laurenslatest.com/lasagna-cups/', 'Lasagna cups');">Lasagna cups</a>!</p>
<p><a href="http://www.laurenslatest.com/lasagna-cups/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.laurenslatest.com/lasagna-cups/', '');"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8799" alt="Lasagna Cups" src="http://www.goodenessgracious.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Lasagna-Cups.jpg" width="400" height="267" srcset="http://www.goodenessgracious.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Lasagna-Cups.jpg 400w, http://www.goodenessgracious.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Lasagna-Cups-300x200.jpg 300w, http://www.goodenessgracious.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Lasagna-Cups-200x134.jpg 200w" sizes="(max-width: 400px) 100vw, 400px" /></a>If you would prefer more traditional lasagna for Italian night, check out this <a href="http://www.goodenessgracious.com/2010/10/goode-gooseberry-project-slow-cooker-lasagna.html">Slow Cooker Lasagna</a> or this quick and easy <a href="http://www.goodenessgracious.com/2010/03/goode-goodeness-lasagna-in-a-pot.html">Lasagna in a Pot</a>.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter" alt="" src="http://www.goodenessgracious.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/DSC_9395_4434.jpg" /></p>
<p>And it wouldn&#8217;t be a complete Italian dinner without some Tiramisu! Tina at My Life As A Mrs. doesn&#8217;t let you down with her sure-to-please <a href="http://www.mylifeasamrs.com/2011/07/tiramisu.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.mylifeasamrs.com/2011/07/tiramisu.html', 'Tiramisu');">Tiramisu</a>!</p>
<p><a href="http://www.mylifeasamrs.com/2011/07/tiramisu.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.mylifeasamrs.com/2011/07/tiramisu.html', '');"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8800" alt="Tiramisu" src="http://www.goodenessgracious.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Tiramisu.jpg" width="500" height="333" srcset="http://www.goodenessgracious.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Tiramisu.jpg 500w, http://www.goodenessgracious.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Tiramisu-300x200.jpg 300w, http://www.goodenessgracious.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Tiramisu-200x133.jpg 200w" sizes="(max-width: 500px) 100vw, 500px" /></a></p>
<p>And there you have it! Your virtual Italian Dinner is all ready to go! Enjoy!</p>
