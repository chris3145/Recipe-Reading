<p>I love eating pie. Sweet or savoury it doesn’t matter as long as it comes piping hot and full of flavor I am willing to burn the inside of my mouth off for one.</p>
<p><img src="http://www.almondtozest.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/IMG_0749.jpg" alt="Chicken, Leek and Brie Pie - Comfort food never tasted so good with leeks cooked to a sweet caramelized state, mixed with chunks of juicy chicken pieces!" width="100%" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://www.almondtozest.com/chicken-leek-brie-pie/" data-jpibfi-post-title="Chicken, Leek and Brie Pie" ></p>
<p>The only problem with making pies though is that they have a fiddley and difficult process that involves different types of pastries and complicated steps that I just don’t have time for most days. But then I realised that there was an easier way, which I’m going to show you today so that you can enjoy the pleasure of eating pie whenever you want, without it taking up your whole day.</p>
<p><img src="http://www.almondtozest.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/IMG_0740.jpg" alt="Chicken, Leek and Brie Pie - Comfort food never tasted so good with leeks cooked to a sweet caramelized state, mixed with chunks of juicy chicken pieces!" width="100%" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://www.almondtozest.com/chicken-leek-brie-pie/" data-jpibfi-post-title="Chicken, Leek and Brie Pie" ><img src="http://www.almondtozest.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/IMG_0743.jpg" alt="Chicken, Leek and Brie Pie - Comfort food never tasted so good with leeks cooked to a sweet caramelized state, mixed with chunks of juicy chicken pieces!" width="100%" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://www.almondtozest.com/chicken-leek-brie-pie/" data-jpibfi-post-title="Chicken, Leek and Brie Pie" ></p>

<p>Leeks cooked to a sweet caramelized state are mixed with chunks of juicy chicken pieces, swimming in a pool of white wine and salty stock. Topped with slices of creamy centered Brie cheese that melts into a gooey mess. This is what makes the glorious filling for this tasty pie.</p>
<p><img src="http://www.almondtozest.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/IMG_0757.jpg" alt="Chicken, Leek and Brie Pie - Comfort food never tasted so good with leeks cooked to a sweet caramelized state, mixed with chunks of juicy chicken pieces!" width="100%" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://www.almondtozest.com/chicken-leek-brie-pie/" data-jpibfi-post-title="Chicken, Leek and Brie Pie" ></p>
<p>The filling is then encased in a couple of sheets of puff pastry and then popped in the oven. I love watching puff pastry transform into its risen form of light crispy layers and its pale complexion tan up to a golden brown glow. And the best bit about this pie is the slits on the top layer that give you a sneaky peak of the glossy filling waiting to ooze out with the first bite.</p>
<p><img src="http://www.almondtozest.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/IMG_0766.jpg" alt="Chicken, Leek and Brie Pie - Comfort food never tasted so good with leeks cooked to a sweet caramelized state, mixed with chunks of juicy chicken pieces!" width="100%" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://www.almondtozest.com/chicken-leek-brie-pie/" data-jpibfi-post-title="Chicken, Leek and Brie Pie" ></p>
<p>To make my pie life easier I opt to use pre rolled frozen puff pastry sheets. Without these I would not be able to eat pies as often as I like, but of course if you have the time you can make your own pastry. Serve with a simple side salad and enjoy for either lunch or dinner. Enjoy.</p>
